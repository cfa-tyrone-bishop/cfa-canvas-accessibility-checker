Canvas Accessibility & Settings Tool

Version 1.0.0

This LTI tool helps instructors and course designers ensure their Canvas courses meet accessibility standards and best practices. Developed by the Learning Design & Innovation Team at CFA Institute.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive accessibility auditing against WCAG 2.1 guidelines
  • Automated scanning of pages, assignments, and announcements
  • Detailed issue reporting with remediation suggestions
  • Customizable scan configurations and notification preferences
  • Exportable reports in multiple formats

Resources

User Documentation Accessibility Guide FAQ Contact Support

Privacy & Compliance

This tool processes course content locally and does not store or transmit sensitive information outside your Canvas instance. All scans and reports comply with FERPA and institutional data policies.
